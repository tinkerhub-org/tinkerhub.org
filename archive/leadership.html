Leadership Workshops

Bring Out the leader in You

TinkerHub belives in people. Building the necessary soft skills for people enables them to take on the most pressing challenges and issues of the 21st century society. The workshops are intended to give exposure about the socio economical changes happening around thereby helping create the leaders of tommorow. The attendees of the workshops will also be added to the tinkerhub information network. This would serve as channel to communicate Informations pretaining to oppurtunities like Fellowships,Scholarship and hackathons so that you never miss anything. Another key deliverable of the workshops emphasis on how one can work with all the different platforms available to build a quality profile. These are of great importance in todays scenerio as a better profile will ensure chances of selection to different opportunities like fellowships and scholarships.
